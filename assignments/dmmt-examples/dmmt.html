DMMT Examples

Bad Website Examples

pennyjuiceupdated.com

This site violates the rules of encouraging users to muddle through their websites. This site fails to guide the user to the purpose of this website. Ams I here to buy juice? Is this a charity page for children? Why is the site so naked, is this website even official?

cameronsworld.net

Cameron's world lacks clear structure and organization, making it difficult for users to understand the content and navigate the site.

greatdreams.com

Great Dreams' website displays inconsistent design elements that can confuse users and fail to establish a clear purpose for the site. Is this a blog? Or is this suppose to be pure satire?

Good Website Examples

clemson.edu

Clemson University's website demonstrates clear navigation with a simple, consistent menu structure that makes it easy for users to find information. The users know where they are at and what they are looking for on every page.

zennioptical.com

Zennioptical's design of their website made a strong highly optical (no pun intented) clear and easy to understand. The purpose of this website is to sell glasses and they make it clear that is what they are doing.

neetcode.io

Neetcode's website make it very clear on their intention and the rule of making users feel like they are in control. It's tree structure allows for easy navigation on what lessons the user would like to learn and they can easily navigate from lesson to lesson.
